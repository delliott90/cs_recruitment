What's The Gameplan?
If you want to join the computer science program at the University of New Brunswick then you need to take the steps required to get there. You should start as early as you can get have a plan to gain all the knowledge you need before you apply. Computer science is like any university degree in that it requires a lot of work and dedication to complete and to do well at. CS it is far from being impossible, and it is not too hard for the average person. Anybody with the right game plan can be successful in computer science.
Keep Your Grades Up
© NEC Corporation of America
The University of New Brunswick asks for a minimum average grade of 75%, but any applicant can apply with an average of 60% or above. The most important part of your grades is not that they are good but that you know and understand how study and how to succeed in your work. You do not need to be a genius to take computer science, but a little hard work goes a long way, and any university degree requires good study habits and little bit of hard work to do well that. You can always do better, and it will never hurt to get good grades, you’ll be surprised how much things you learn in the past make more sense in the future. Keeping your grades up works in every game plan we have in life!
Get Awesome at Math
© Ben Andreas Harding
Math is important to computer science. Math is the logic brings computer science to life. Computer work at their core by doing math and it helps to understand a little how it all works. Math can be challenging and computer science is not all 1s and 0s or multiplying and dividing, but you’ll be required to take a few classes that deal with concepts you’ve been learning your whole life. Math is fun and it’s a science in its self. Sign up for an advance math course and get learning, and you will be excited to see how it can apply to your life. If math is not your strongest subject, do not worry, there is expect help around and friendly people who can help explain it and walk you through. Add getting awesome at math your game plan and you will succeed!
Take a Programming Course
© Jiuguang Wang
If you think computer science is something you would be interested in, or you would like to learn more about how it all works, take a programming course. Programming is not as hard you think, and it is not boring. Programming lets you create something and you get to see that come to life on your screen. Programming can open up the world to you! Sign up at your school for a programming course, or take one of many course online for free. You can start programming at any level and start learning. A good programming course can start you off with something simple and before you know it you will be doing something complex and others will be impressed. If computer science is something you think you might find interesting than adding a programming course to your game plan is the best place to start!

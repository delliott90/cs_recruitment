Careers & Salaries
Whatever your dream job, CS can get you there.
Behind any piece of software is a Computer Scientist. Software can be small or almost invisible like the software that moves a robotic arm, or large and complex like whats used in virtual reality but is every case people are tasked with building, testing, installing, maintaining, and working with that software. Since software is so diverse, so are the jobs involved, and if you can dream it, CS can get you there.
Medicine
© NEC Corporation of America
The main goal of software has always been to improve people’s lives. This can be the most evident in the medical field. Computer Science is credited with saving many lives. Computer science can work on machines that help diagnose and treat illnesses. Computer science is always essential in medical research and laboratory work, at home tracking of personal health, and the day to day working in a hospital or doctor’s office. Without computer science, there is no modern medicine.
Video Games
© Ben Andreas Harding
The fun part of computer science can be making video games. If you think playing video games is fun, making your own or being part of a large scale game. Game design can be fun and rewarding to help create. Computer science could create everything from graphics, to background logic, to game controls, and everything in between. If video games is something you love, computer science the field of choice.
Robotics
© Jiuguang Wang
Software can do amazing things, and it can do more than just display words on a screen. Software can move the world, and it can move the Canadian Arm attached to a space shuttle. Some believe robotics is the future, and all those machines need software to tell there how to move. If the hardware of a robot is it’s body, the software created by a computer scientist is it’s heart and soul. Robotics is an exciting field and can it really can take you out of this world.

Software Development
© hackNY.org
Software Development is the open world where dreams become an reality. Anything you can think of you can work to develop it into software. Software development can be just you doing everything from the start to the finish or you working as a part of a team in a large complex system. Software development can create something new or bring older system up to date. Software development has no limits.
Mobile Apps
© Ashok Govind
Mobile application makes approximately $30 billion dollars a year, and maybe you could make some of that money with your idea. Mobile apps are small, simple and fun to bring you simple idea to the small screen. There is a large demand for mobile developers in the job market. Like every company has to have a website, every company now has to have a mobile application. Everything in computer science seems to be moving mobile, and you could be part of it.
